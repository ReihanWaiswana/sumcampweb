About Us

Supporting local bakery UMKM through a modern online catalog.

Our Story

Sweet Crumbs was created to help local bakery businesses introduce their products to more customers. We believe every bakery has a unique story, homemade recipes, and quality products that deserve a wider audience through digital technology.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become a trusted digital platform that empowers local bakery UMKM and connects them with customers.

Mission

  • Promote local bakery products.
  • Provide an attractive online catalog.
  • Support UMKM digital transformation.

Our Values

Quality, Freshness, Creativity, Customer Satisfaction, and Community Growth.
